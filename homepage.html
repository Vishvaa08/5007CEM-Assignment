IS THERE A SHORTAGE?

Yes there is a major blood shortage issue! This is caused by many factors such as...

☛ Lacking knowledge of donation
☛ Lacking resources and facilities
☛ Lacking cultural norms to donate


Who Can Donate?

In most states, donors must be age 17 or older. Donors must weigh at least 50KG and be in good health.

How Often Can I Donate?

You must wait at least 8 weeks between donations of whole blood and 16 weeks between Power Red donations.

How Long Is The Process?

The entire process takes about an hour and fifteen minutes, the actual donation of blood takes about 8 to 10 minutes.
